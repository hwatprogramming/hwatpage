The Problem:

In many jobs, schedules are used to communicate to workers the days in which they need to work.

These schedules are planned to fulfill the job requirements and work-health regulations.

However, there are times where these schedules may not satisfy each worker's individual preference and may cause them to be unhappy.

This may occur because the planner may not understand what the work is like as they might not have done it before.

Also, the planner might be inexperienced and may not understand how to plan a good schedule.

The Goal:

I want to create a schedule generator that generates schedules that fulfill job requirement and work-health regulations.

After that, it will take the worker's preference into consideration and figure out the best schedules to implement.

The General Idea:

First, the user will input the required details into a form.(shifts, workers and requirements/limitations)

Second, the software will generate all the possible combinations based on the requirements and limitations.

Third, the software will scan all the schedule combinations and score them based on certain factors(based on worker preferences).

Fourth, the software will sort the schedule combinations and display best combinations on top.

Fifth, the user can also input some information into a a blank schedule to find matching schedule combinations.

Finally, the user can export the schedule into an excel or google sheet file.
